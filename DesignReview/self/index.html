Design Review

Kousik's Webpage

Font

Rating: 7.5/10

  • Webpages showcased good font consistency, but some lacked proper hierarchy and spacing.
  • Solutions improving line height, font weight, and spacing were noted as excellent improvements.
  • CSS Zen Garden template was often overlooked, affecting layout consistency.

Color

Rating: 7/10

  • Dark and light themes were executed well, but gradient blends needed refinement in some cases.
  • Color switches were overwhelming in some designs, and alignment issues were noted on smaller screens.

Icons

Rating: 7.5/10

  • Icon grids and hover effects were implemented effectively but lacked clarity in labels and color contrast.

Images

Rating: 7.5/10

  • Full-bleed headers and lazy-loaded images demonstrated a good understanding of image optimization.
  • Several designs lacked accompanying content or context, affecting the overall layout.
  • Improved image properties and compression were well-documented in notes.

Sample Website

Rating: 8/10

  • Minimalist designs with proper hierarchy.

Design in Practice

Rating: 7/10

  • Redesigns often improved navigation, readability, and spacing, addressing clutter in original designs.
  • Annotations and reasoning were helpful but could benefit from more clarity and detail.
Overall Rating: 7.5/10
