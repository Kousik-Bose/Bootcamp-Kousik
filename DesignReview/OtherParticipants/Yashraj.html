Yashraj Singh's Design Review

Review

The navigation system feels somewhat inconsistent and could be improved. Navigating back to the homepage can be unnecessarily complex, creating some confusion for users.

EX-1: Font

Rating: 7.5/10

The fonts are implemented well with good functional design. However, the content layout feels slightly unclear in terms of user location within the design. Adjusting the paragraph line height would enhance readability.

EX-2: Color

Rating: 8/10

The layout is clean and visually appealing, but the monochrome theme is underutilized. The gradient version, however, was an impressive highlight with smooth and elegant styling.

EX-3: Icons

Rating: 9/10

Great use of both SVG and Font Awesome icons. The integration and styling were skillfully done, enhancing the overall design appeal.

EX-4: Images

Rating: 7/10

Image placement and styling were clean and professional. Margins and padding were well-used, creating a refined and polished look.

EX-5: Sample Website

Rating: 8.5/10

Excellent replication of the Medium article page. The layout and use of the CSS box model were well executed and visually accurate.

EX-6: Design in Practice

Rating: 8.5/10

The redesign of the IITM CS faculty and IRCTC pages was creative and visually appealing. Minor refinements could make the experience even stronger.

Overall Rating: 8.5/10
