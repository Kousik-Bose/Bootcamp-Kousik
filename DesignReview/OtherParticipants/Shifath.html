Design Review

Shifath's Webpage

Review Summary

Shifath's webpage showcases a strong understanding of design principles with minor areas for improvement. The overall execution is clean and effective.

EX-1: Fonts

Rating: 8.5/10

The font choices were well-thought-out, providing excellent spacing and typography. The background color complemented the fonts effectively. However, there was a minor issue with an inaccurate title in one section.

EX-2: Colors

Rating: 8/10

The color palette was cohesive and visually appealing. While the choices were easy on the eyes, some areas could include more context about the reasoning behind the palette. The gradient used on a button could also be refined.

EX-3: Icons

Rating: 8/10

The icons were seamlessly integrated and well-utilized. However, adding clearer explanations and improving the color schemes for some icons would enhance their impact. The icon grid could benefit from better labels.

EX-4: Images

Rating: 8.5/10

The use of images was effective, with responsive designs and well-chosen alt text. The lazy-loading feature was a great addition. Adding more detailed context about the image properties would further enhance the presentation.

EX-5: Sample Website

Rating: 8.5/10

The article layout was clean and easy to navigate. The information was presented effectively and was simple to follow. Overall, it demonstrated a well-executed design.

EX-6: Design in Practice

Rating: 8/10

The redesigns highlighted a solid understanding of design principles. The annotations were helpful in explaining the reasoning behind choices. However, some redesigns would benefit from improved image selection and better color schemes.

Overall Rating: 8/10
