Design Review

Shilpa's Webpage

Review Summary

Shilpa's webpage demonstrates a good understanding of design principles with room for improvement in providing additional context and refinement for some elements. The overall design is clean and functional.

EX-1: Fonts

Rating: 8/10

The font choices were effective, especially with the use of the CSS Zen Garden template in Level 1. A consistent application of the template throughout the exercise would enhance clarity and continuity.

EX-2: Colors

Rating: 8.5/10

The color palette was cohesive and well-implemented, creating a visually appealing and user-friendly experience. The design was intuitive and easy to follow.

EX-3: Icons

Rating: 7.5/10

The icons were utilized effectively to enhance the design. Adding more context and better integration with the content would provide a more polished result.

EX-4: Images

Rating: 7/10

The images were presented cleanly but lacked sufficient explanation or context. Providing more details about the image properties and their usage would improve the overall quality.

EX-5: Sample Website

Rating: 8.5/10

The minimalist article layout was thoughtfully designed, with no distractions and excellent use of spacing. The second website's card-based layout was an excellent touch, combining visuals with concise content effectively.

EX-6: Design in Practice

Rating: 7.5/10

The redesign of the IRISET/IRCTC websites showed potential but felt somewhat cluttered. There was little guidance for user navigation. However, the reasoning behind identifying the flaws in the original design was well-articulated.

Overall Rating: 8/10
