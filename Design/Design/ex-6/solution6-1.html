Website Improvements for Government & Educational Sites

Analysis of Existing Websites and Suggested Inspirations

1. Indian Government Press Releases

Current Problems:

  • Outdated design with cluttered text.
  • Hard to navigate with no search or filter options.
  • No mobile responsiveness.

Suggested Improvement:

  • Adopt a clean, modern design like the US Department of State's website.
  • Implement filters and search options for easier content access.
  • Ensure mobile responsiveness and a visually appealing design.

US Department of State

A clean and organized press release section with filtering options and responsive design.

2. IITM CSE Faculty Site

Current Problems:

  • Overwhelming text with no clear visual hierarchy.
  • No profile images or multimedia to engage visitors.
  • Hard to scan faculty details and research information.

Suggested Improvement:

  • Use a layout similar to Stanford University's faculty pages.
  • Add profile images, concise biographies, and categorized information.
  • Include multimedia like research videos or project links.

Stanford University CS Faculty

Organized faculty profiles with clear sections and images.

3. IIT B Talk

Current Problems:

  • Cluttered layout with too many elements competing for attention.
  • Hard to discover content like speaker information and event schedules.
  • No modern layout or responsive design for mobile devices.

Suggested Improvement:

  • Use a grid-based layout for speakers, talks, and event schedules, inspired by TED Talks.
  • Optimize for mobile responsiveness and easy content discovery.
  • Make the website visually appealing with a modern, minimalistic design.

TED Talks

Clean grid-based layout and easy-to-navigate content sections.

4. Indian Railways Website

Current Problems:

  • Cluttered interface with too much text and information.
  • Inconsistent design elements and hard-to-find information.
  • Slow load times and lack of interactive features for booking and schedules.

Suggested Improvement:

  • Streamline the design and improve content structure, like Amtrak's website.
  • Add interactive features such as live train schedules and a booking system.
  • Ensure mobile responsiveness and improve loading speed.

Amtrak

Modern, user-friendly design with booking functionality and live information.
